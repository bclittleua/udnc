Featured selections:

Cat's Cradle by Kurt Vonnegut, jr.
A fictional/prophetical novel by a very enlightened individual. A religion in itself masked behind a brilliant story.

"If I were a younger man, I would write a history of human stupidity... and lie down on my back with my history for a pillow... lying on my back, grinning horribly, and thumbing my nose at You Know Who."
Kurt Vonnegut Index
The Tao of Pooh by Benjamin Hoff
An instructional booklet about the further unraveling of life's "mysteries". Considered a bible by some cultures.

"When you wake up in the morning, Pooh," said Piglet at last, "what's the first thing you say to yourself?"
"What's for breakfast?" said Pooh. "What do you say, Piglet?"
"I say, I wonder what's going to happen exciting today?" said Piglet.

Pooh nodded thoughtfully.
"It's the same thing," he said.
The Tao of Pooh
