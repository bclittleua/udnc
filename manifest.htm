The United Disorder of Nonsense and Chaos v1.5a


  1. The Law of Self Importance
      You are not important.
      Not one person is better than you.
      You are no better than anyone else.
                  Listen to yourself. Fail at your own hands.

  2. The Rules of Engagement
      Finish what you started.
      Ignoring a problem leaves a hole in your defense.
      Destroy all weeds at the root.

  3. The Law of Probable Dispersal
      Duck.
      Anything thrown into a fan will not be evenly distributed.
      Avoiding the shit will save you the shower.

  4. The Way of Life
      There is no happiness without suffering.
      Fight your suffering, and you will be subjected to it's fury.
      Embrace your suffering, and it will show you it's rewards.

  5. The Secret to Success
      The Enemy with nothing to lose, should be the Enemy most feared.
      Never get into fist fights with ugly people.
      Never steal from a poor man.

  6. The Answer
      Decide your own Fate.
      You are the only help you are really ever going to get.
      You will not know what you like, until you find out what you don't.
